About Pet Valet

Pet Valet is a dog walking service. A dog owner can call for a dog walk and have a walker arrive in minutes, with the transaction occurring on the mobile website, for a flat rate of 15 dollars an hour. You can also sign up to be a dog walker and earn money for playing with fluffy critters. Dog walkers earn 10 dollars per hour.

Pet Valet was developed by Kathryn Moran and Carolyn Mei during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Microsoft Chicago, and they are very excited to welcome you to use their service.

About Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization which aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science. The organization runs summer immersion programs which teach computing and programming skills to high school girls.
