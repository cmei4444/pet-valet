About Pet Valet

Pet Valet is essentially a dog walking service in which a dog owner can call for a dog walk and have a walker arrive in minutes, with the transaction occuring on the mobile website.

Pet Valet was developed by Kathryn Moran and Carolyn Mei during the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Microsoft Chicago, and they are very excited to welcome you to use their service.

About Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a nonprofit organization which aims to support and increase the number of women in computer science. The organization runs summer programs which teach computing and programming skills to high school girls.
